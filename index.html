About

UnemployCoin ($UNEMP) is a decentralized ERC-20 token built on the Polygon network. Our mission is to give power back to the unemployed through blockchain-based tools like resume vaults, job directories, and community-driven projects.

Project Vision

We're building a decentralized resume database — resumes stored on-chain, searchable by employers, powered by $UNEMP.

Why? Because finding a job shouldn't mean giving away your data to corporations who ghost you anyway.
