UnemployCoin™ is built on the Polygon network, a secure and cost-effective Ethereum Layer 2. With lightning-fast transactions and ultra-low fees, it’s the ideal foundation for a decentralized movement focused on inclusion.

Mission Statement

UnemployCoin™ exists to challenge the status quo of economic exclusion. In a world where traditional systems overlook the unemployed and underrepresented, we believe it's time for a different kind of currency—one rooted in empathy, transparency, and decentralization. Our mission is to create a financial ecosystem that gives power back to the people. Through blockchain technology and a fair, community-owned token, UnemployCoin™ provides digital tools and support to those left behind by traditional finance. Built on the fast and affordable Polygon network, UNEMP allows anyone with an internet connection to participate, contribute, and grow. Whether you’re jobless, underpaid, or simply fed up, this coin represents solidarity, not speculation. We are not here to promise wealth. We’re here to build a movement.

Faq

Here are some of the most common questions about UnemployCoin, its mission, and how you can get involved. 

What is UnemployCoin™?

UnemployCoin™  is a community-driven cryptocurrency designed to support unemployed individuals with dignity, transparency, and decentralized tools. It’s more than a token—it’s a statement of solidarity.

Why was this created?

Born from frustration with broken systems, UNEMP gives power back to the people. Our goal is to turn digital currency into real-world opportunity—no gatekeepers, no middlemen.

Is UnemployCoin™ safe to use?

Yes. The token is deployed on the Polygon network using audited ERC-20 standards. Ownership is fixed, and the contract is fully visible on GitHub for transparency.

How can we help?

UnemployCoin™ exists to create a decentralized safety net for people left behind by the system. Whether you're here to contribute, ask questions, or learn more, we’re ready to connect. Explore our open-source tools, view the live contract, or reach out to join the community.
